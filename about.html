ABOUT ME.

Hello, I'm Ashley!

I'm a journalism student in the Medill School at Northwestern University, also pursuing a minor in Spanish.

I spend half my time designing, illustrating, coding, and scoping out cool people to be in my projects. I spend the other half of my time trying to convince myself that I'll eventually get used to the Chicago winters. Apparently, it's a work in progress.

I've been a Gold Member of Starbucks since 2011, I'm on the hunt for the best medium-rare burger in the world, and I sometimes dream that Ira Glass is narrating my life.
