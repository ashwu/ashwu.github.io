NORTH BY NORTHWESTERN

BREWING BAD

As digital producer for NBN in winter 2015, I designed and developed online web layouts to accompany the quarterly print magazine. I coded interactive elements for five stories (including one about home-brewed beer), added and edited editorial content and created original illustrations. More stories can be found here.

SPOON UNIVERSITY

SPOON UNIVERSITY - PRINT MAGAZINE

Spoon is a national publication and network dedicated to food and dining for college students. Northwestern University's print magazine has an audience of over 2500. As Creative Director, I manage the design team, plan and direct photoshoots, as well as design pages and create illustrations. Our most recent winter issue was released March 9, 2015.

SIDE PROJECT

ROMAINE

Working with three other Knight Lab student fellows (Mallory Busch, Suyeon Son and Nicole Zhu), we reimagined the way Northwestern students could search for and choose their courses each quarter. We used Parse as a backend and worked with Northwestern Course System and Facebook APIs to create a social way to select classes.

CLASS PROJECT

BEING FIRST

Using the Adobe Digital Publishing Suite, I created an interactive iPad app for my feature on first generation college students. I conveyed the story through clean and functional design, original reporting, photography and videography.

CLASS PROJECT

HOW EBOLA COMPARES

I created an alternative story for a Media Design class comparing Ebola to three other potentially deadly diseases: HIV/AIDs, influenza and rabies. I thought a structured chart with illustrations breaking the grid would be the most efficient way to create easily digestible content that conveyed useful information.

CLASS PROJECT

BETWEEN THE LINES

As the final project for my Advanced Interactive Storytelling and Design class, I helped report, write, develop and design a story on the use of modern technology in fighting traditional book censorship.

DESIGNWORKS

NU MODEL UNITED NATIONS PAMPHLET

DesignWorks is an organization that is commissioned by student groups and academic departments on campus to create promotional materials. The cover for the Northwestern Model UN Delegate Guide was my first project as a senior designer.

EDGEVILLE BUZZ

THORNDALE MURAL

In late 2013, Green Star Movement started a new project to create a mural under the Thorndale CTA station in Edgewater, Chicago. I reported on a meeting that the organization had with their student volunteers for an Enterprise Reporting in Diverse Communities class and created a video that was published in a local news site, Edgeville Buzz.

NORTH BY NORTHWESTERN

HINMAN BALL PIT

I shot, edited and produced a video about Freshmen roommates that built a ball pit in their dorm room after they received more that 2,500 hits on Reddit. The video remains one of the most watched videos of North by Northwestern.
