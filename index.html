Hi I'm Ashley!
(Ashwu if you're into nicknames)

I'm a recent graduate of Northwestern University with a journalism major and a Spanish minor. I currently do design and development as a Knight Lab student fellow. Some other gigs have been with the Los Angeles Times graphics team and the LA NACION data team in Buenos Aires.

As you can probably tell, I'm interested in design, graphics and data visualization. I also enjoy watching Food Network, wearing fun socks, speaking Spanglish (but also regular Spanish), eating family-style at restaurants and listening to this song to get me through some those rough Mondays.
